Single Journey Ticket (SJT)

Single Journey ticket holders have to travel by Shuttle buses only.

SJT is charged @ of Rs.70 per day (to & from) irrespective of bands.

Yearly Journey Ticket (YJT)

To avail a route bus service, please register a Yearly Journey Ticket (YJT ) through EASY.

YJT Cancellation Procedures

Cancellation of YJT shall be raised by the associates in EASY and submit the ticket to CS (Location Transport-In-Charge) within stipulated time (24hrs from the time of cancel request registered).
